# FTK2: Research References

The architecture and algorithms of FTK2 are based on the following research publications:

## Core Framework
*   **FTK: A Simplicial Spacetime Meshing Framework for Robust and Scalable Feature Tracking**  
    Hanqi Guo, et al. *IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics*, 2021.  
    [arXiv:2011.08697](https://arxiv.org/abs/2011.08697)  
    *Primary insight: Simplicial spacetime meshing for robust tracking.*

## Feature Extraction Algorithms
*   **Exact Analytical Parallel Vectors (ExactPV)**  
    Hanqi Guo, et al. *arXiv preprint*, 2021.  
    [arXiv:2107.02708](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.02708)  
    *Primary insight: Analytical cubic rational solver for exact parallel vector tracking.*

## Distributed & Parallel Tracking
*   **A Distributed-Parallel Algorithm for Connected Component Labeling in Spacetime Meshes**  
    Hanqi Guo, et al. *arXiv preprint*, 2020.  
    [arXiv:2003.02351](https://arxiv.org/abs/2003.02351)  
    *Primary insight: Asynchronous label propagation for distributed-parallel CCL.*

## Advanced Meshing
*   **Feature Tracking in Deforming Spacetime Meshes**  
    [Student's Paper], *arXiv preprint*, 2023.  
    [arXiv:2309.02677](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.02677)  
    *Primary insight: Non-linear extrusion and feature tracking on evolving grids.*
